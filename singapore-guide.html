下一步建議

Recommended Next Steps

在採取行動前，先理解自己的身份與目標

Before taking action, clarify your residency status and investment goals

1
釐清您在新加坡的身份
Clarify your Singapore residency status
您是否計劃申請就業准証（EP）或永久居留權？這將對銀行開戶及置業稅率產生決定性影響。
Are you planning to apply for an Employment Pass (EP) or Permanent Residency? This is the single most important factor affecting both banking and property tax.
2
非居民銀行開戶門檻
Non-resident banking threshold
如無 EP，三大銀行非居民理財戶口門檻均為 SGD 35 萬（約 TWD 805 萬）；建議在開戶前先諮詢銀行關係經理。
Without EP, the threshold at all three major banks is SGD 350,000 (~TWD 8.05M). Consult a bank relationship manager before proceeding.
3
置業優先考慮商業物業或 REIT
Prioritize commercial property or REITs
如以置業為目的，優先考慮商業物業（如店屋）或上市房地產信託（REIT）——兩者均無須繳付 ABSD，對外籍人士最為友善。
If investing in property, prioritize commercial assets (shophouses) or listed REITs — both have 0% ABSD and are fully open to foreigners.
4
諮詢持牌專業人士
Consult licensed professionals
在作任何決定前，務必咨詢新加坡持牌律師及稅務顧問，以確保符合最新法規。TIREA 可協助您對接新加坡合作律師及房產顧問。
